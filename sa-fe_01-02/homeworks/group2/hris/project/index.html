About us

Vocal quartet ‘ABAGAR’ at Konstantin Preslavsky University of Shumen is a young creative formation, formed by the voices of the young and talented girls - Kalina, Stefka, Sofia and Hristina. They became famous and favorites of the audience in a very short time for their exclusive style belonging to an unusual aspiration of originality in stage performance. ‘ABAGAR’ vocal quartet is already known in the concert life of the country for its participation in master classes of the most prominent and world famous Bulgarian singers. They take part in different national forums. Recent soloists of the quartet are Galina Zamfirova and Ekaterina Kostadinova – both vocal students. Guest soloists are - Anna Natskova and Diyana Vasileva - sopranos, formar students in the National School of Folklore “Philip Koutev”. Desislava Petrova – soprano in the National Folklore Ansamble “Philip Koutev” and Nevyana Ganeva from Folklore Ansamble “Trakia” - Plovdiv also perform with the quartet as guest vocalists. Conductor - Sashka Chenkova / Petrova/
