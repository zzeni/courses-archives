Walkthrough of the site

Our website allows hardcore tv show fans to enjoy their favorite shows with a one-click search. Binge-watching has never been more fun!

Want to watch a show?

Head over to the Search tab in the navigation and search for what suits your chill today!

Want to track your favorite shows?

Log on and add the shows that suit your overall chill to your chill list. Every time you log on, you can see and edit your chills according to your mood.

Don't know when your show's latest episode is released?

Every time a new episode of a show in your chill list has come out, a mail will be sent to you to let you know it's chill time!

Suit your mood today!

Our search allows you to find shows you want to watch right now!
