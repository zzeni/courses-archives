Paneurhythmy

Today Paneurhythmy is practiced all over the world: Argentina, Australia, Bulgaria, Canada, Great Britain, Ireland, France, Germany, Mexico, Philippines, Russia,Costa Rica, USA, and many others. Thousand of people come to the summer camp at the Seven Lakes in the Rila Mountains, Bulgaria to perform Paneurhythmy and enjoy the majesty of the mountain.
